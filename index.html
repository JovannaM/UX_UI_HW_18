I am a United States Air Force Veteran and a Fine Arts graduate with newly gained UX and UI design skills currently living in Charlotte, North Carolina.

My UX Skills

User Research

These skills consist of a variety of practices in the Discovery and Definition phases of User Research. To produce efficient research and analytics I conduct User Surveys and Interviews with a variety of specific participants, and conduct a Competitor Analysis that will eventually help build a User Persona and Storyboard.

User Interface Design

These skills consist of a variety of practices in Ideation, Design and Testing phases. In order to strategically plan on a design I study website Heuristics for redesigns and have participants participate in card sorting activities. Based on User Research I develop User Flows which will then contribute to Low Fidelity Wireframes. I conduct Usability Tests to make iterations for the final mockup.

Web Development

Basic knowledge in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Experience in VS Studio Code and Github software.
